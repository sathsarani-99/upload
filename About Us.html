About Cafe

Coffee beans and brew cafe is a one of the leading coffee shops situated in Colombo.Cafe typically serve veriety of coffees beverages and short eats. Currently there are two branches and bussiness is growing rapidly.

Our History

The shop started in 2014 with small number of people. But because of the increasing demand for the cofee currently they have another branch in Colombo 7 and 16 employees are working in both branches. Next year there will be another branch opens at Colombo 3. The shop facilitates a sitting area for the customers as well as food and beverages to take out. In the past shop was operated at-counter service.But now customers can order online and the coffee will be available at thier door step.
